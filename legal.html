Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Marcus Jenkins Coaching ("I," "me," or "my") is committed to protecting your privacy. The men who reach out to me are often sharing deeply personal things. I take that trust seriously, and I handle your information accordingly. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your data when you visit this website or engage my coaching services.

By using this site, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of this site.

Information We Collect

I collect information in the following ways:

  • Personal Data: Your name, email address, phone number, and any other contact details you provide when booking a discovery call, submitting the contact form, signing up for email communications, or purchasing coaching services.
  • Coaching Session Information: Notes and records related to our coaching work together, which are kept strictly confidential and never shared with third parties.
  • Payment Information: Billing and payment details processed through our secure third-party payment processors. I do not store your full payment card information on my servers.
  • Usage Data: Standard analytics about how you interact with this website — pages visited, time on site, device type, and referring sources — used solely to improve the website experience.
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to support site functionality and remember your preferences. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function as intended.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide, operate, and deliver coaching services
  • Process bookings and transactions and send related confirmations
  • Respond personally to your inquiries and messages
  • Send email communications you have opted into (you may unsubscribe at any time)
  • Improve the quality and relevance of this website and its content
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

I do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes — ever.

Coaching Disclaimer

Marcus Jenkins Coaching provides faith-based personal development coaching. Coaching is not therapy, counseling, psychiatry, or any form of licensed mental health treatment. I am not a licensed therapist, psychologist, or medical professional.

My work is designed to help men identify root-level patterns, develop a healthier relationship with their identity in Christ, and build practices that support lasting transformation. It is not designed to diagnose, treat, or cure any mental health condition, including addiction or trauma disorders.

Results from coaching vary based on individual effort, circumstances, history, and commitment to the process. Testimonials shared on this site reflect the real experiences of specific men and are not guarantees of outcomes for every client.

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, suicidal ideation, or acute trauma symptoms, please contact a licensed mental health professional or call the National Crisis Line at 988 immediately. Coaching is not a substitute for clinical care.

I do encourage clients who are in therapy to continue that work alongside coaching — the two can complement each other well. If you have questions about whether coaching is appropriate for your situation, please reach out before booking.

Refund Policy

I am committed to the men I work with, and I want every client to feel they made the right investment. Refund requests are handled on a case-by-case basis within the first 30 days of program enrollment.

If, within 30 days of starting, you feel the program is not the right fit for reasons within my control, reach out to me directly at marcus@marcusjenkinscoaching.com. We will have an honest conversation and work toward a fair resolution. I will not hide behind a policy if something went wrong.

Refunds are not available after 30 days or for missed sessions due to client scheduling conflicts. Discovery calls (free) and the free training carry no purchase obligation and no refund is applicable.
