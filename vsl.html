What You'll Learn

Four Truths the Church

By the end of this training you will understand exactly why you've been stuck — and have a clear picture of the way out.

01
The Real Root Beneath the Behavior

This is not a lust problem or a discipline problem. I'll show you exactly what has been driving the behavior from underneath — and why it makes complete sense that willpower, filters, and accountability groups have never worked long-term.

Your Host

Meet Marcus Jenkins.

I'm Marcus Jenkins. I used pornography for 26 years while serving as a church deacon, leading men's ministry, and building a corporate finance career. I tried every program — Every Man's Battle, Covenant Eyes, Celebrate Recovery, X3Pure, accountability partners, pastoral counseling, and men's groups. I'd get clean for 30 days. Then I'd relapse. Shame spiral. Back to the same confession. For 26 years, that was the hidden architecture of my life.

In 2021 I met a coach who took me back to an 8-year-old boy standing in a kitchen watching his dad leave. In that session, something unlocked that 26 years of behavioral approaches never touched. Three years free — not as a personal achievement I'm proud of, but as the natural outcome of actual healing. This training is the conversation I desperately needed 20 years ago and couldn't find anywhere.
